Chapter 1: Introduction

Weve mentioned in the previous target that there are two personas in this project - An educator, who can create courses. And students who can sign up, browse available courses, and enrol in courses that interest them. They can then access lessons within their enrolled courses. The following format of describing features is called a "user story" in agile development. We write user storie s from the perspective of different personas in a project.
